What is all this?

Pickem Pickem is just a small web site designed to make it easy and enjoyable to participate in and manage NFL "pickem" pools.

And what’s a “pickem” pool?

Get a bunch of friends, coworkers or strangers together in a pool, throw in some money, and pick a single winning NFL game each week. If you pick incorrectly, you're out. The last person standing takes the pot. That's a "pickem" pool. Simple, right?

Okay, I’m intrigued

It's dirt simple to create a new pickem pool. Just sign in with your Facebook account below to get started.

A note about money & gambling

You'll have to manage the money for each entry in the pool on your own. We have no idea what kinds of hoops we'd have to jump through in order to let you manage that money through this site, and we don't much want to find out at the moment. Sorry about that. We do try to make all the other aspects easy, though!
